## A Quieter Side of Limaru

Sun Moon Lake is a scenic stop for players who want to slow down, take in the landscape, and see how Limaru builds beyond its busiest urban areas.

Use it as a relaxed first outing, a photography stop, or a destination to pair with nearby towns and transit routes.
